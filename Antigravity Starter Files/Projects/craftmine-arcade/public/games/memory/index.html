How to Play

  • 🃏 Click any card to flip it face up
  • 🔍 Flip a second card to try to find a match
  • ✅ Matching pairs stay face up
  • ❌ Mismatches flip back after 1 second
  • 🏆 Match all 8 pairs to win!
  • ⚡ Try to win in as few moves as possible
